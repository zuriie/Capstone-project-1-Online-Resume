The Best Movies According to Lenora

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Medical Series

If it in anyway revolves around the hospital, then i'm game.

Crime Series

Especially if it is Legal, Cop or Crime investigation centered.

Action

Everything Fast & Furious and Transporter: Or better still, if the likes of Arnold Schwarzeneggar, Jason Statham, Dwayne Johnson, Van Damme etc are in it, then it is surely going to be interesting.
